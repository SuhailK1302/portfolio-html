The Best Tamil Movies According to Suhail

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time in Tamil

1.Enthiran

Release Year: 2010
Director: S. Shankar
Lead Actors: Rajinikanth, Aishwarya Rai Bachchan
Genre: Science Fiction, Action, Romance

Introduction:

Endhiran, also known as Robot, is an iconic Tamil movie that took the world by storm upon its release in 2010. Directed by the visionary filmmaker S. Shankar, the movie stars the legendary Rajinikanth in dual roles as Dr. Vaseegaran, a brilliant scientist, and Chitti, a sophisticated humanoid robot. Aishwarya Rai Bachchan plays the female lead as Sana, Vaseegaran's love interest.

Plot Summary (Avoiding Spoilers):

Endhiran revolves around the life of Dr. Vaseegaran, a robotics expert, who creates an advanced and humanoid robot named Chitti. Initially designed to serve humanity, Chitti develops human-like emotions and falls in love with Sana, leading to unforeseen consequences. The movie explores the complexities of human emotions and the ethical dilemmas of artificial intelligence.

Acting and Performance:

Rajinikanth's performance is nothing short of a tour de force. His ability to effortlessly switch between the roles of the stern scientist and the playful robot is commendable. Aishwarya Rai Bachchan's portrayal of Sana adds charm and grace to the film.

Direction and Cinematography:

S. Shankar's direction is impeccable, showcasing his mastery in blending science fiction elements with emotional depth. The movie's extravagant and visually stunning sequences elevate the cinematic experience, making it a visual spectacle.

Screenplay and Dialogue:

Endhiran's screenplay keeps the audience engrossed with a perfect blend of action, romance, and humor. The dialogues are memorable, with Rajinikanth's punch lines becoming legendary in Tamil cinema.

Music and Songs:

The music, composed by A. R. Rahman, is a highlight of the film. The songs, especially "Kilimanjaro" and "Enthiran," remain etched in the minds of the audience even after years of its release.

Final Verdict:

Endhiran is a groundbreaking and revolutionary film that set new benchmarks in Tamil cinema. Rajinikanth's charismatic performance, S. Shankar's visionary direction, and A. R. Rahman's soul-stirring music make this movie a must-watch for all cinema lovers. Endhiran's success lies not only in its entertainment value but also in its thought-provoking exploration of the human-robot relationship. It continues to be an enduring classic that has left an indelible mark on the history of Indian cinema.

2.Super Deluxe

Release Year: 2019
Director: Thiagarajan Kumararaja
Lead Actors: Vijay Sethupathi, Samantha Akkineni, Fahadh Faasil, Ramya Krishnan, Mysskin
Genre: Drama, Thriller

Introduction:

Super Deluxe is an unconventional and thought-provoking Tamil movie that left audiences captivated upon its release in 2019. Directed by Thiagarajan Kumararaja, the film weaves together multiple interconnected stories, exploring themes of redemption, acceptance, and societal norms.

Plot Summary (Avoiding Spoilers):

The movie consists of four intertwined narratives that converge in unexpected ways. A transgender woman named Shilpa (played by Vijay Sethupathi) returns home after a long absence, seeking acceptance from her family. Another storyline follows Arputham (played by Mysskin), a devoted husband who stumbles upon a shocking discovery. A couple, Leela (played by Samantha Akkineni) and Vaembu (played by Fahadh Faasil), find their relationship tested when faced with a momentous event. Meanwhile, an ex-porn actress, Manickam (played by Ramya Krishnan), struggles to come to terms with her past.

Acting and Performance:

The stellar performances by the ensemble cast are the movie's highlight. Vijay Sethupathi, Samantha Akkineni, Fahadh Faasil, Ramya Krishnan, and Mysskin deliver powerful and emotionally charged portrayals, elevating the movie's impact.

Direction and Cinematography:

Thiagarajan Kumararaja's direction is masterful, skillfully weaving the complex narratives together. The movie's unconventional storytelling style and non-linear structure keep the audience engaged and intrigued. The cinematography, led by P. S. Vinod and Nirav Shah, is visually striking, complementing the film's tone and atmosphere.

Screenplay and Dialogue:

The screenplay is well-crafted, delving deep into the complexities of human relationships and societal norms. The dialogues are sharp, poignant, and thought-provoking, providing a glimpse into the characters' emotions and inner conflicts.

Music and Background Score:

The film's music, composed by Yuvan Shankar Raja, adds depth and emotion to the narrative. The background score enhances the mood, intensifying the impact of key scenes.

Final Verdict:

Super Deluxe is a cinematic gem that defies conventional storytelling, leaving a lasting impact on its audience. The movie's daring exploration of diverse themes, coupled with brilliant performances, make it a must-watch for cinephiles seeking a compelling and unconventional cinematic experience. Super Deluxe challenges societal norms and offers a unique perspective on human relationships, leaving the viewers with thought-provoking questions long after the credits roll.

Thuppakki

Release Year: 2012
Director: A. R. Murugadoss
Lead Actors: Vijay, Kajal Aggarwal
Genre: Action, Thriller

Introduction:

Thuppakki is a high-octane action thriller that took the audience by storm upon its release in 2012. Directed by A. R. Murugadoss, the film features Vijay and Kajal Aggarwal in lead roles.

Plot Summary:

The story revolves around Jagadish (Vijay), an army captain on vacation, who stumbles upon a terrorist plot while visiting his family. He decides to take matters into his own hands to thwart the terrorists' evil plans and protect the city of Mumbai from an impending threat.

Acting and Performances:

Vijay delivers a powerful performance as Jagadish, effortlessly portraying the character's charisma and action-packed sequences. Kajal Aggarwal shines in her role as Nisha, bringing charm and warmth to the screen. The chemistry between Vijay and Kajal adds an extra layer of emotion to the narrative.

Direction and Action Sequences:

A. R. Murugadoss's direction keeps the audience on the edge of their seats throughout the film. The well-crafted action sequences, choreographed by Anal Arasu, are a highlight of Thuppakki, showcasing Vijay's martial arts skills and thrilling stunts.

Music and Background Score:

The music, composed by Harris Jayaraj, is a perfect fit for the film's adrenaline-pumping moments and romantic interludes. The song "Google Google" became a massive hit, adding to the film's overall popularity.

Final Verdict:

Thuppakki is a gripping action thriller that keeps the audience engaged from start to finish. With a compelling storyline, stellar performances, and breathtaking action sequences, it is a must-watch for fans of action-packed cinema. A. R. Murugadoss's direction and Vijay's charismatic portrayal of Jagadish make Thuppakki a memorable and enjoyable cinematic experience.
